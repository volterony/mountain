Liam Irving is a highly qualified and experienced outdoor professional who has been working in the outdoors for 10 years. Liam holds qualifications in winter mountain leadership, climbing, canoeing, kayaking, mountain biking and many more. Originally from the west coast of Scotland, he is now based in Aviemore where he climbs, kayaks and surfs as often as possible. liam@cairngormadventureguides.co.uk
Dan Goodwin has been working, skiing, climbing and mountaineering in the Scottish Highlands for thirteen years. He holds qualifications up to the MIC training. His climbing and mountaineering began as a child and has seen him climb, ski and work from Yosemite, the Alps, the Andes and Himalaya. The Scottish winter remains his passion be it climbing, skiing or just enjoying a walk across the tops. He has written for the country's most popular climbing and mountaineering website UKclimbing.com presenting their weekly winter news and conditions report. You can see some more on Dans personal blog by clicking here, dan@cairngormadventureguides.co.uk
Rob Jarvis IFMGA Guide and MIC. Based in the Chamonix valley Rob has come through a traditional mountaineering background and still holds a passion for Scottish winters. Robs climbing and mountaineering experience runs from gritstone to many seasons climbing and guiding in the Scottish Highlands, Alpine climbs like the Eigers North Face and beyond to the greater ranges.
Rocio Siemans, MIC and Trainee IFMGA Guide. Rocio has a wealth of climbing and mountaineering experience and climbs to high grades on rock and ice, as well as being an excellent skier. Rocio has climbed and skied all over the world  and her passion and knowledge for mountaineering ensure you will thoroughly enjoy her courses. She is also an ambassador for Adidas and DMM.
Nick Carter MIC. Nick has extensive experience as a climber and instructor. With the first ascent of 'Hung Drawn and Quartered' VII,8, new routes in the Peruvian Andes and the Khumbu region in the Himalaya, Nick's personal experience runs to a very high standard. As an instructor, Nick's passion and enthusiasm for mountaineering is contagious and you will leave a course delivered by Nick feeling inspired.
Andy Wardle MIC. Andy has a great passion for the mountains and has spent time climbing in the UK and Alps to a high level. A former Officer in the British Army and subsequent years instructing and guiding in the Scottish Highlands, Andy has a relaxed and confident approach which facilitates learning in a safe and professional way. Andy is held in high regard within the mountaineering community and industry.
Andy Spink MIC,IML. Andy Spink has twenty years of instructing experience and has spent time climbing throughout the UK, Alps and greater ranges. His experience also sees him at the forefront of developing good practice on aspects within the outdoor industry. Andy has a huge passion for Scottish mountaineering whether its during the winter or summer, and can often be found exploring new climbs throughout the Scottish Western Isles. Andy's instructing and personal experience ensure a very high standard.
